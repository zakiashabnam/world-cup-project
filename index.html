The FIFA World Cup, often simply called the World Cup, is an international association football competition contested by the senior men's national teams of the members of the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA). The world's first international football match was a challenge match played in Glasgow in 1872 between Scotland and England, which ended in a draw. The first international tournament, the inaugural British Home Championship, took place in 1884.
